Suhas Tamvada

San Jose, CA

  • I grew up in Hyderabad, India, where I got my Mechanical Engineering B.Tech degree and optimized automobile head restraints to reduce whiplash effects in rear end collisions as part of my undergraduate thesis.
  • I then moved to Chicago, IL for my Master's to study fluid interface interactions with implications to oil spills, emulsions, and targeted drug delivery.
  • Later, I got my PhD at University of Florida, researching thermal transport at interfaces and developing data center cooling applications.
  • A bay area internship during my PhD opened my eyes to the fascinating world of building products. I found more joy in shipping a product than publishing a paper, and it led to my current role at Tesla, where I work on thermal management of power electronics.
  • My current interests include nuclear energy, diffusion models in machine learning, cryptography, and quantum computing. Please reach out if you want to talk more!
